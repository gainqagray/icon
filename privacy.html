Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pardon ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information in compliance with the Google Play Developer Policies.

1. Data Collection & Transparency

We collect only the minimum data necessary for the core functionality of the App:

  • Personal Data: Email addresses (sender and recipient) for message delivery and authentication.
  • User Content: Text inputs provided by the user to generate apology messages via AI.
  • Technical Data: Device model and OS version via Firebase for stability and performance monitoring.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted partners to provide our services:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication and data management.
  • Brevo: For email delivery services.
  • AI Processing: For generating text based on your prompts.

3. Data Deletion & User Rights

In accordance with Google Play requirements:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data via the App settings at any time.
  • Manual Requests: Contact us at support@pardon.app to request permanent data removal.

4. No Sale of Data

We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. Your information is used strictly to provide the Pardon service.

5. Security

All data is transmitted securely using TLS/SSL encryption and stored with industry-standard protection via Firebase.
