Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pardon ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information. We comply with the Google Play Developer Distribution Agreement and all applicable data protection laws.

1. Data Collection & Transparency

We collect only the minimum data necessary for the core functionality of the App:

  • Personal Data: Email addresses of the sender and recipient to facilitate communication.
  • User Content: Text inputs provided by the user to generate messages. This content is processed in real-time.
  • Device Information: We collect non-sensitive technical data (OS version, device model) via Firebase to monitor app performance and crashes.
Note on Sensitive Permissions: Pardon does not access your contacts, location, camera, or microphone without your explicit, real-time permission.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

To ensure high-quality service, we partner with the following entities:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication and real-time database management.
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): As our SMTP provider to deliver emails to your designated recipients.
  • AI Processing (OpenAI/Google): For generating text. We do not use your personal identifiers for AI training purposes.

3. Data Security & Encryption

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS/SSL).
  • We utilize Firebase Security Rules to ensure that users can only access their own data.

4. Data Deletion & User Rights

In accordance with Google Play requirements, we provide a clear path for data management:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the "Settings" menu in the App.
  • Manual Request: You may contact us at support@pardon.app to request a full export or permanent deletion of your data from our systems.
  • Automatic Deletion: We do not store generated messages longer than necessary for delivery and history logs.

5. No Sale of Personal Data

We strictly do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing firms. Your data is used exclusively to provide the Pardon service.

6. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Pardon is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

7. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please reach out to us:

Email: support@pardon.app
