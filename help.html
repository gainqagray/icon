Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send a message?

Simply enter the recipient's email, write your message, and hit send. We handle the delivery for you.

Is it private?

Yes. You can choose to send messages anonymously. We do not share your personal info with the recipient unless you want to.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings within the app and select Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.
