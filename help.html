Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a message?

You can describe your situation to our AI assistant to generate a sincere apology, or you can choose to write your message manually for a personal touch.

How is the message delivered?

Once your message is ready, it is sent directly to the recipient's email address. We ensure secure and reliable delivery.

Can I remain anonymous?

Yes. You have the option to send your apology anonymously. The recipient will see the message but not your personal contact details if you choose this option.

How do I delete my data?

Your privacy is important. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings menu within the app.
