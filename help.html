Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a message?

You have full flexibility: you can choose to use our AI help to generate a sincere apology text based on your situation, or you can simply use your own words to write the message manually.

How does Pardon work?

Pardon assists users in drafting effective apology messages using advanced technology, making it easier to find the right words for difficult moments.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use Firebase for secure authentication and do not share your private messages with third parties for marketing purposes.

Can I remain anonymous?

Yes, you can choose to send apologies anonymously. The recipient will see the message but not your personal identity.
