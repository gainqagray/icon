Data Safety

Safety starts with understanding how developers collect and share your data. Data privacy and security practices may vary based on your use, region, and age.

🛡️
Data Encrypted in Transit Your data is transferred over a secure connection (TLS/SSL) and is not accessible to unauthorized parties.
🗑️
Data Deletion Available The developer provides a way for you to request that your data be deleted via account settings or support email.
🚫
No Data Shared with Third Parties We do not sell your data to brokers or advertisers. Data is only shared with essential service providers (Firebase, Brevo) to perform app functions.

Data Collected

  • Personal Info: Email address (Required for Account Management and App Functionality).
  • User Content: Other user-generated content (Text used for generating apologies).
  • App Performance: Crash logs and Diagnostics (Used for analytics and app health).
  • Device Identifiers: Device or other IDs (Used for Firebase authentication).
